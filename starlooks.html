STAR LOOKS

Star Looks: The Life and Death of a Star

For this project, I designed eight unique outfits, each representing a different phase in the stellar evolution cycle.


These outfits illustrate both the life cycle of a sun-like star and the more complex stages of evolution for massive stars. For sun-like stars, I covered phases from the main sequence to the white dwarf phase, including the red giant stage.


For massive stars, I included phases such as the supernova explosion and the resulting neutron star or black hole formation. Each outfit is crafted to visually embody the physical characteristics and transformations that occur in each stellar phase.
